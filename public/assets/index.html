Why Tracks Adventure Safaris

COMPANY BACKGROUND

Tracks Adventure Safaris & Lodges is a business that was incorporated under the companies act in Maun situated in the North West District. It was formed to satisfy the needs of fellow countrymen and international travelers by providing facility management services and assisting the government in its mandate of public private partnership.

Tracks Adventure Safaris & Lodges is 100% Botswana owned company with intentions of breaching the gap in the facility management service providers by making a difference. Tracks Adventure Safaris & Lodges is located in the new mall at plot 361 along the airport road opposite the museum. It is a business led by a dedicated, hardworking and passionate team and it is fully owned by Ms. Onalenna Phillimon.

Tracks Adventure Safaris & Lodges is a new mobile safari business company and it is in its formative stage which was created to take advantage of a specific gap in the local market. The gap for mobile safari business exists in high quality, reasonably price mobile safari in Maun which is entirely own by a Motswana woman as stated above.
